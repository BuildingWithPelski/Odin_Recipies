Lasagna


Description

A lasagna meal is a hearty, baked Italian casserole featuring layers of wide pasta sheets alternating with rich sauces (like tomato-meat ragù or creamy béchamel), cheeses (ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan), and sometimes vegetables or sausage, resulting in a savory, bubbly, and comforting dish perfect for sharing

Ingredients

  1. Noodles:
    • Lasagna noodles(regular or oven-ready)

  2. Meat Sauce:
    • Ground Beef, Italian Sausage or a mix
    • Onion, Garlic, Bell pepper
    • Tomato Sauce, Crushed tomatoes, Tomato paste
    • Italian Seasoning, basil, salt pepper

  3. Cheese filling:
    • Ricotta cheese
    • Mozzarella cheese
    • parmesan cheese
    • Egg (to bind)
    • parsley, salt, pepper

Directions:

  1. Make the Sauce: Sauté onion/garlic, brown the meat, drain the fat, then simmer with tomatoes and seasonings.

  2. Prepare the Cheese Filling: Mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, and seasonings in a bowl.

  3. Cook Noodles Boil regular noodles until al dente; skip if using oven-ready.

  4. Layer:Spread sauce on the bottom, then noodles, then cheese filling, then more sauce, repeat layers, and finish with plenty of mozzarella and Parmesan.

  5. Bake: Bake covered, then uncovered (or broil briefly) until bubbly and browned.
